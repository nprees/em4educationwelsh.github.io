Manifesto

Exams and Coursework

Fight against exams and coursework deadlines being on the same day.

Extenuating Circumstances

Provide students with a better overview of the extenuating circumstance process and guidelines.

Set Seminars

No more set seminar timetables, you have the option to choose the seminar you want.

Lecture Recording

Make it compulsory for lectures to record all of their lectures.

Gender Stereotypes

Campaign against gender stereotypes within the University.

Timetables

Ensure no more issues occur with the Publish timetabling software.

First Aid

Provide first aid days across all colleges.

Union Events

No more lectures on Union events, such as Varsity.

Charity Funding

Build relationships with charities to help students with funding their studies.

Changes and Absences

Guarantee email notifications to alert students on timetable changes and lecturer absences.

Book Availability

Provide more online textbook access, as well as discounted book.

Charging Lockers

Implement charging lockers to provide secure devices charging storage.

Drop in Sessions

Fortnightly drop in sessions with both myself and your subject representatives.

Study Areas

Improve and expand the library and study areas, especially on the Bay Campus.

Student Experience

Focus on improving the student experience for those struggling with mental health.
